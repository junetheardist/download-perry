Perrry Global Cooperative

Perry Global Cooperative is a cooperative tech brand that enables members of ethnic minorities to achieve their financial goals through real estate property investments as a group.

Perry provides an online marketplace and community where people can connect with each other, learn about property investment opportunities, and invest in real estate collectively.

Our mission is to promote social justice by empowering minority communities through education and opportunities.
